How it works:

This site is designed to make a decision for you, when you can't figure out what to do tonight. By imputing your preferences for tonights activities you allow us to make sure the choice is right for you. If you don't like the choice, no problem just hit refresh and you will have a new suggestion right in front of you based on the same peramiters you set.
